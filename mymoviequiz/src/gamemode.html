Category

How It Works

You're in charge! Choose whatever movie category you wish and all questions would be about that category, for example imagine you have selected 'Action' then the questions could be about films like 'First Blood (Rambo)', 'Terminator', 'The Matrix', and many others examples, however be aware that one movie could have many categories so in the example above 'Terminator' may appear in 'Action' and 'Sci-fi' category as well.

Daily Challenge

How It Works

Welcome to the Daily Challenge! It's time to face the real test and prove that you're the best of the world, at least for one day. In this mode you'll receive a game with 4 packs, each pack contains 10 questions that were randomly selected, answer as fast as you can and earn the top spot. At the end of the contest the player with highest score wins. It's simple, it's fun and you should start right away!
